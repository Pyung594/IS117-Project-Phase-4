Weekly Newsletter (Updated 11/28/2022)

My Market Outlook and Trade Ideas For The Week

Outlook for S&P 500 Futures/SPY

The S&P 500 has been in a range since Friday, November 11th of 2022. I expect this range to break soon with a move below 3937.50 or above 4050.75 on S&P 500 futures (Ticker: $ES1!).

As we are at the high of this range currently near the 4050.75 level, I will most likely be bearish and look for short entries on a move down unless we see buyers step in and pressure bears above this level.

Range trading is tricky as there is lots of choppiness and consolidation towards the center of the range, which we must be aware of to trade it reasonably. If going short, go short at the highs of the range, and if going long, go long at the lows of the range.

We have lots of news this week coming out like non-farm payrolls and Federal Reserve speeches, so I believe volatility will come into the market soon because this consolidation makes for non-ideal trading conditions.

Netflix ($NFLX)

Netflix has been on quite a run in recent days! It has pulled back to the golden zone once and bounced immediately, which gives me the idea that institutional buyers are present at that area. However, the market has since been a little weaker and whatever gains Netflix could hold onto were erased.

But, if Netflix can find buyers to rally over the $290 price level, we may see interest pick up and I believe a short squeeze can be generated with this structure.

The target for this trade would be for the price gap at $300-$303 to be filled and then we can look for continuation to the highs at around ~$312. This trade will be completely invalid below the $275 price level.
